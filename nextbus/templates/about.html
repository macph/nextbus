About

This website

This is a personal project.

As a long-time bus user, I have often been frustrated by how difficult it is to plan around bus timetables and the lack of access to live, up to date information for buses, especially on mobile. I created this website to gain experience in building websites and to provide a service.

GitHub repository: github.com/macph/nextbus

Data sources

  • National Passenger Transport Gazeetter—region, administrative area and locality data. Published by the DfT and licensed under the Open Government Licence (OGL).
  • National Passenger Transport Access Nodes—stop point data. Published by the DfT and licensed under the OGL.
  • National Statistics Postcode Lookup—postcode data. Published by Camden Open Data and licensed under the OGL. Contains OS data Â© crown copyright and database right 2018.
  • National Operator Code—service operator data. Published by Traveline.
  • Traveline National Dataset—service data. Published by Traveline and licensed under the OGL.

Transport API is used to obtain live bus stop data.
